Our Events

Art Exhibition

May 29, 2025 @ 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Join us for a captivating art exhibition hosted by "Helping-Hand Foundation", showcasing diverse artworks that highlight important social and environmental themes. Through this event, we aim to inspire dialogue and raise awareness about pressing issues facing our world today. The exhibition features works by talented artists who use their creativity to advocate for change and promote positive impact. Join us for an evening of artistic expression and meaningful conversations as we explore how art can drive social transformation. Don't miss this opportunity to engage with thought-provoking art and be part of our mission to create a more compassionate and sustainable future through creativity.

Key Features:
  1. Art Exhibition: The main attraction will be a diverse display of artworks from emerging and established artists, showcasing paintings, sculptures, photography, and mixed-media pieces. Each artwork will reflect themes of cultural diversity, unity, and inclusivity..
  2. Interactive Installations: Engaging interactive installations will invite attendees to participate actively in the artistic experience, fostering a sense of connection and dialogue.
  3. Artist Talks: Scheduled artist talks and discussions will provide insights into the creative process, inspirations behind the artworks, and the role of art in promoting social change.
  4. Workshops: Hands-on workshops led by artists and NGO representatives will encourage attendees of all ages to explore their creativity and express themselves through art.
  5. Community Engagement: The exhibition will feature collaborations with local community groups, highlighting stories and perspectives from diverse cultural backgrounds.
  6. Panel Discussions: Thought-provoking panel discussions involving artists, activists, and scholars will delve into topics such as multiculturalism, identity, and the intersection of art and social activism.

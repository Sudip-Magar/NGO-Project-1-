Our Events

Film Screening

MAY 1, 2025 @ 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Join us for an inspiring film screening event hosted by "Helping-Hand Foundation", where we showcase thought-provoking documentaries and films focused on critical social and environmental issues. Through this event, we aim to raise awareness, spark meaningful conversations, and inspire action within our community. The screening will be followed by an engaging discussion, providing attendees with an opportunity to share insights, ask questions, and explore ways to contribute to positive change. Don't miss this chance to be part of a powerful cinematic experience that drives impact and promotes collective understanding. Together, let's make a difference through film and dialogue.

Film Screening:
  • Film Title: Shaping Tomorrow: A Journey of Impact
  • Director: Sudip Magar
  • Duration: 2.5hr
Key Features:
  1. Opening Remarks: The event will commence with opening remarks from representatives of "Helping-Hand Foundation", providing context for the film and its relevance to the NGO's mission.
  2. Film Screening: Attendees will enjoy a screening of "Shaping Tomorrow: A Journey of Impact", which explores themes such as "Community-driven change for a sustainable future.", shedding light on important societal issues.
  3. Post-Screening Discussion: Following the screening, there will be a moderated discussion with guest speakers, including filmmakers, activists, or subject matter experts. Attendees are encouraged to participate and share their insights
  4. Audience Q&A: An interactive Q&A session will allow attendees to pose questions to the panelists and delve deeper into the film's themes and impact.

Our Events

Funds research

MAY 15, 2025 @ 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Join us for an impactful fundraising event hosted by "Helping-Hand Foundation", dedicated to supporting groundbreaking research initiatives. Our event aims to raise vital funds for innovative research projects focused on addressing critical health, environmental, or societal challenges. Through your participation and generosity, we can advance scientific discoveries and propel solutions that have the potential to transform lives and create positive change. Together, let's make a difference by investing in research that fuels progress and fosters a brighter future for all. Don't miss this opportunity to contribute to impactful research and join us in our mission to drive meaningful impact through knowledge and innovation.

Key Features:
  1. Welcome Reception:
    • Guests will be welcomed with drinks and hors d'oeuvres, providing an opportunity to network with researchers, donors, and community members.
  2. Dinner Program:
    • Opening Remarks: Representatives from "Helping-Hand Foundation" will introduce the organization's mission and the importance of funding research to tackle pressing challenges.
    • Keynote Address: Listen to a distinguished expert in the field discuss the urgency of addressing the specific challenge and the impact of research on finding solutions.
  3. Research Showcase:
    • Researchers supported by the NGO will present their projects and findings through poster sessions, interactive exhibits, or brief presentations.
    • Attendees will have the chance to engage directly with researchers, learning about the latest breakthroughs and potential impacts of their work.
  4. Silent Auction and Fundraising Activities:
    • Silent Auction: Bid on unique items, experiences, and services donated to raise funds for research initiatives.
    • Live Fundraising Appeal: Encourage attendees to contribute to specific research projects through a live fundraising appeal, highlighting the potential impact of their donations.
  5. Recognition and Appreciation:
    • Donor Acknowledgment: Recognize and thank donors for their contributions to advancing research efforts.
    • Researcher Recognition: Showcase the dedication and achievements of researchers funded by the NGO, highlighting their impact on addressing critical challenges.
